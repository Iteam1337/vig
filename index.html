Welcome to the visual world of Iteam.

Here you can find some guidelines created to help build the new look and feel for Iteam.

1.0
Our logotype

Our logotype is our visual core. It is a representation of our company and our primary sender. It has a unique and characteristic expression which makes us stand out in the crowd.

1.1 Versions

There are three versions of logotype: a vertical lockup, a horizontal lockup and a symbol.

Use the symbol in smaller formats where the logotype loses in readability or in cases when Iteam is written out in conjunction with the symbol.

1.2 Logotype colors

We only use the logotype in black or in white. Try to ensure maximum visability.

1.3 Clearspace

To ensure maximum visibility there should always be a clearspace around the logotype. The recommended amount of clearspace is shown below.

Its distinct character lends itself superbly to be read across platforms, and is distributed by Google Fonts.

6.0
Applications

Here you can find some of our applications together with some thoughts on their design.

Some things are always good to keep in mind when working with the identity:

  • Think about what you want the application that you are designing to achive. Is it meant to grab attention or maybe just present information in the clearest possible way?
  • Be consistent with placement and sizes of typography and logotype over a range of material. Reuse settings from other designs if possible.
  • Always align elements to each other to create harmony and structure.
  • Don't complicate things. Keep it simple and trust the identity!
